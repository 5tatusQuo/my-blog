## What to Expect

This blog will be a platform where I share:

- **Technical insights** from my journey as a software developer
- **Tutorials and guides** on web development, React, Next.js, and other technologies
- **Project showcases** of things I'm building
- **Thoughts on technology** and its impact on our lives

## Why I Started This Blog

Writing has always been a powerful way for me to:
- Solidify my understanding of complex topics
- Share knowledge with the community
- Document my learning journey
- Connect with like-minded developers

## The Tech Stack

This blog is built with:
- **Next.js 15** - For the framework
- **Tailwind CSS** - For styling
- **Contentlayer** - For content management
- **MDX** - For rich content creation
